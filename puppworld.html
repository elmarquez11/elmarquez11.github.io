Some Facts: 6.2 million puppies are born each year in the U.S. The most popular dogs are Labrador retrievers and German Shepherds. Puppies are born without teeth. Puppies require 5 meals per day. A one year puppy is equivalent to a 15-year-old human. Puppies don't open their eyes until 9-12 days old.

Some sad Facts: More than 50% of the fur America gets from China are from dog and cats that have been skinned alive. The common abuse dogs go though are neglect and abuse. About half of all the dogs that end up in the shelter end up put down because they remain unclaimed. Leaving a dog in the car or outside on a hot day for as little as 15mins can cause brain damage. Most dogs are left in the shelter for being too old once a dog is "too old" they are taken to the shelter where they will most likely be put down because of age.
